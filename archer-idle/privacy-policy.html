Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Archer Idle mobile game application (the "App") published by Vinarto Games.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, to provide and improve our services, we and our third-party service providers may automatically collect certain information when you access and use the App. This includes:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, user-resettable identifier for advertising provided by your device's operating system (e.g., Android Advertising ID).
  • Device Information: Data regarding your device model, operating system version, and hardware specifications.
  • IP Address: Used to determine your approximate location based on your network connection.
  • App Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the App, such as session duration, progression, and feature usage.
  • Diagnostic Data: Crash logs, performance metrics, and other diagnostic data to help us troubleshoot issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the game, allowing us to improve gameplay mechanics and user experience.
  • Stability and Performance: To detect, investigate, and resolve crashes and technical bugs.
  • App Functionality: To manage app configurations and deliver remote updates to game behavior without requiring a manual app store update.
  • Advertising: To deliver relevant advertisements to you within the App (when enabled).

3. Third-Party Services

The App utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or your device. These services have their own privacy policies governing the collection and processing of your data. We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config

4. Advertising

We have integrated Google AdMob into the App. While advertisements may not be actively displayed at all times, the AdMob SDK is present and may use your device's Advertising ID and IP address to serve ads. Depending on your region and device settings, these advertisements may be personalized based on your interests or non-personalized.

You can manage your ad personalization preferences or reset your device's Advertising ID directly through your Android device's system settings (usually under Settings > Google > Ads).

5. Data Safety and Sharing

In alignment with Google Play's Data safety requirements, we disclose the following regarding your data:

  • Data Sharing: The automatically collected usage, device, and diagnostic data is shared with Google (via AdMob and Firebase) strictly to facilitate analytics, crash reporting, and advertising delivery.
  • No Data Sales: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Data Security: Data collected by our third-party partners is transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption protocols.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately so that we can take the necessary steps to remove that information from our systems.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
