Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Archer Idle mobile game application (the "App") published by Vinarto Games.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, third-party services used in the App may automatically collect certain information.

  • Advertising ID: A unique identifier used for advertising.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, hardware info.
  • IP Address: Used to estimate general location.
  • App Usage Data: Gameplay interactions, session duration, progress.
  • Diagnostic Data: Crash logs and performance data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve gameplay.
  • Stability: To detect and fix crashes and bugs.
  • App Functionality: To manage configurations and updates.
  • Advertising: To display ads via third-party services.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or your device.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config

For more information, please visit:

  • https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • https://support.google.com/admob
  • https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as Advertising ID, IP address, and device information.

Depending on your region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we may request your consent before showing personalized ads. You can change your preferences at any time through your device settings.

5. Data Safety and Sharing

  • Data Sharing: Data may be shared with Google services for analytics and advertising.
  • No Data Sales: We do not sell your personal data.
  • Data Security: Data is transmitted securely using standard encryption.

6. Data Retention

We retain collected data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your data. You may contact us for any such requests.

8. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

9. Third-Party Disclaimer

We do not control third-party services and their data practices. Please review their privacy policies for more information.
