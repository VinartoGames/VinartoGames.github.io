This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Archer Idle mobile game application (the "App") published by Vinarto Games.

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the App and third-party services may automatically collect certain information, including:
