Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Mage Survivor mobile game (the "App") published by Vinarto Games.

1. Information Collection

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number. However, the App integrates third-party SDKs that may collect limited data automatically for analytics, performance, and advertising-related purposes. This may include:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, hardware details.
  • IP address: Used for basic network functionality and security.
  • App usage data: Gameplay actions, session duration, and progress.
  • Diagnostic data: Crash logs and performance information.
  • Advertising ID: If provided by the device.

No user accounts are created, and we do not store personal data on our own servers.

2. Use of Information

Collected information may be used for:

  • Improving gameplay experience and game balance.
  • Detecting and fixing crashes and performance issues.
  • Maintaining app stability.
  • Analytics and understanding general usage trends.
  • Supporting advertising and monetization systems through third-party SDKs.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect and process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Unity (Game Engine and services)
  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (advertising SDK integrated)
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config

These services may collect device identifiers and usage data independently of the App. For more information, please review their respective privacy policies.

4. Advertising

The App includes the Google AdMob SDK. Advertising features may not be active for all users at all times, but the SDK may still collect data required for potential ad functionality.

AdMob may process data such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Device information

Ads, when enabled, may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user settings and regional regulations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. However, limited data may be shared with third-party service providers (such as Google and Unity services) for analytics, crash reporting, and advertising-related functionality.

6. Data Retention

We do not store user data on our own servers. Any data collected is retained according to the policies of the third-party services used in the App.

7. User Accounts and Data Deletion

The App does not require user registration or accounts. All game progress is stored locally on the user’s device.

Users can delete all data by:

  • Clearing app data from device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the App

Since no account system exists, there is no server-side data deletion request process.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is processed by third-party services, it is handled according to their respective policies.

9. International Data Transfers

Data collected by third-party services may be processed on servers located outside the user’s country.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
